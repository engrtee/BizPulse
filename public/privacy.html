1. Who We Are

BizPulse is a WhatsApp-native financial operating system for small business owners. It is operated by The Tosin Ilesanmi Data Academy, a CAC-registered entity based in Lagos, Nigeria.

For any privacy-related enquiry, contact us at tosinilesanmi93@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

When you register and use BizPulse, we collect the following categories of personal and business data:

  • Identity data: Full name
  • Business data: Business name and business type
  • Contact data: WhatsApp phone number and email address
  • Location data: State or region (provided at registration)
  • Financial entries: Daily revenue, expenses and expense categories, customer counts, and inventory movements (items received and sold)
  • Message content: The text of WhatsApp messages you send to BizPulse for the purpose of logging entries
  • Usage data: Entry timestamps, message timestamps, and streak data

We do not collect payment card details, government ID numbers, or location data beyond the state or region you provide at registration.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect only for the following purposes:

  • Parsing your WhatsApp messages to extract financial entries
  • Storing your entries in our secure database to build your financial history
  • Generating your web dashboard showing revenue, expenses, profit, and margin
  • Sending your 7pm daily summary every evening by WhatsApp and email
  • Generating AI-powered business insights using Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude
  • Sending retention nudges when you have not logged for 3, 5, 7, or 14 days
  • Sending milestone celebration messages when you reach streak or entry milestones
  • Providing on-demand summaries and business coaching responses when you request them

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

4. AI Processing

BizPulse uses two AI services: Google Gemini parses your WhatsApp messages into structured financial data. Anthropic Claude generates business insights, coaching responses, and daily summary recommendations.

Only aggregated financial summaries (such as total revenue, total expenses, and margin) are sent to AI services to generate insights. Raw WhatsApp message text is sent to Gemini solely for the purpose of parsing — it is not stored by Gemini or used for model training under our service agreement.

Your personal identifiers (name, phone number, email) are never sent to any AI service. AI processing is performed on anonymised numerical summaries only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We share limited data only with the following service providers, strictly for the purpose of delivering the BizPulse service:

  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API): Delivery of WhatsApp messages you send and receive
  • Google Gemini: Parsing of WhatsApp message text into financial entries
  • Anthropic Claude: Generation of business insights and coaching responses
  • Render.com: Hosting of the BizPulse application and PostgreSQL database
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue): Delivery of summary emails

Each provider processes data only as necessary to perform their function and is bound by their own privacy obligations.

6. Your Rights Under NDPA 2023

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate data we hold
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your financial history as a CSV file at any time via the Settings screen or the /api/export/csv endpoint
  • Objection: Object to processing for specific purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent at any time, which will result in account deactivation
To exercise any right: email tosinilesanmi93@gmail.com — or send the message DELETE MY DATA on WhatsApp to your BizPulse number. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account remains active. If you request deletion of your account:

  • All personal data and financial entries will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request
  • Anonymised, aggregated statistics (such as total user counts) that cannot be linked back to you may be retained

We do not retain data longer than necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Your financial data is stored in an encrypted PostgreSQL database hosted on Render.com
  • All data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • WhatsApp messages are transmitted using Meta's end-to-end encryption
  • Access to the database is restricted to application code running on the same platform

No security system is impenetrable. If you become aware of any security concern, please notify us immediately at tosinilesanmi93@gmail.com.

9. Legal Requests Policy

We only disclose user data to government or law enforcement bodies in response to a valid court order or legally binding instrument issued under Nigerian law or applicable international legal cooperation frameworks.

When we receive such a request, we will:

  • Review its legality and scope before complying
  • Challenge requests that are unlawful, overbroad, or procedurally defective
  • Disclose only the minimum data necessary to comply with the lawful order
  • Notify affected users of any disclosure where we are legally permitted to do so
  • Document all legal requests received and actions taken

We will not voluntarily cooperate with informal or non-binding requests for user data.

10. Cookies

BizPulse uses essential session cookies only. These are necessary for the web dashboard to function (for example, to keep you logged in during a session).

We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Google Analytics or any other analytics platform
  • Meta Pixel or any social media tracking
  • Third-party marketing or retargeting cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may prevent the BizPulse dashboard from functioning correctly.

11. Children

BizPulse is not directed at persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at tosinilesanmi93@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes (changes that affect how we use your data or your rights), we will:

  • Notify all registered users via WhatsApp at least 14 days before the change takes effect
  • Update the effective date at the top of this page

Continued use of BizPulse after a material change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
